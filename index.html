FAQ

Privacy-first means privacy-first.

More at hello@localyze.ai.

Does Localyze.ai send my chats to the cloud?

No. All assistant inference runs on your device. Your prompts, voice, and image inputs never leave the device for the core chat features.

Which model does Localyze.ai use?

Google's open Gemma 4 E4B, running natively on your hardware — packaged in the optimal format for each platform.

How much does Localyze.ai cost?

$89 USD, paid once. No subscriptions, no tier limits. The single purchase covers every feature on Android, Windows, macOS, and Linux.

Does it work fully offline?

Yes. After the one-time model download, Localyze.ai works in airplane mode. Optional features like web search are clearly toggleable.

Which platforms are available right now?

Android and Ubuntu Linux are available today. macOS, Windows, and iOS are in active development and will ship as separate releases — one $89 purchase covers every platform once released.

What hardware do I need?

Android 9+ with ~6 GB RAM. On Ubuntu, any 64-bit machine from the last five years with at least 8 GB RAM.
