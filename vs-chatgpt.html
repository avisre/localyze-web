Where ChatGPT genuinely wins

The honest part.

We are not pretending a 4B-parameter on-device model matches a frontier cloud one. If you need the absolute best answer to a hard, abstract question, ChatGPT is still ahead, and that is fine — different tools for different jobs.

  • Raw model quality on long, complex, multi-step reasoning.
  • DALL·E image generation and the code interpreter sandbox.
  • Plugins, custom GPTs, and a mature third-party tool ecosystem.
  • Team workspaces, shared chats, and admin controls.

Localyze.ai exists for the times that trade-off is not acceptable: confidential client work, regulated industries, fieldwork without a signal, or simply not wanting another account tied to another vendor.

FAQ

Is Localyze.ai a real ChatGPT replacement?

For private chat, voice questions, image questions, summarisation, and notes — yes, it handles them well on modern hardware. For frontier reasoning and DALL·E image generation, ChatGPT is still ahead.

Does Localyze.ai send any data to OpenAI or to Localyze?

No. Inference runs entirely on your hardware. Your chats, voice, and images never reach OpenAI, Localyze, or any third party because there is no server call to make.

Which model does Localyze.ai use vs ChatGPT?

Localyze.ai runs Google's open Gemma 4 E4B locally, packaged natively for each platform. ChatGPT runs OpenAI's closed GPT-4o and GPT-5 family in the cloud.

Can I use Localyze.ai offline?

Yes. After the one-time model download of around 3.6 GB, Localyze.ai works in airplane mode on planes, trains, and remote sites. ChatGPT needs a constant connection.
