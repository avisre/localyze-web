Where Gemini genuinely wins

The honest part.

Gemini is the right tool when your work already lives inside Google. A local model cannot read your Drive, pull live search results, or summarise your inbox, and we are not going to pretend otherwise.

  • Deep Workspace integration across Gmail, Docs, Sheets, and Drive.
  • Real-time Google search grounding for fresh facts and citations.
  • Gemini 2.x multimodal capability at the frontier scale.
  • Tight Android assistant hooks and Pixel device features.

Localyze.ai is for everything else — sensitive notes, confidential drafts, fieldwork without coverage, and anyone who would rather not hand Google another continuous stream of prompts and images.

FAQ

Is Localyze.ai a Gemini replacement?

For private chat, voice questions, image questions, and notes — yes, it covers the same daily ground. For Workspace integration and live Google search, Gemini is still ahead.

Does Localyze.ai run a Google model?

Yes — Google's open Gemma 4 E4B, the same open-weights family Google publishes for the community. The difference is that it runs on your device, not in Google's data centre.

Will my chats be used by Google?

Not with Localyze.ai. Nothing ever leaves your device, so Google, Localyze, and any third party never see your prompts or your responses.

Does Localyze.ai work offline?

Yes. After the one-time 3.6 GB model download, Localyze.ai works in airplane mode on planes, trains, and remote sites. Gemini needs a constant connection to Google's servers.

Keep Gemma. Lose the cloud.

$89 once, native, and offline-first. The same open Gemma 4 E4B model on Android, Windows, macOS, and Linux, with no Google account in sight.
